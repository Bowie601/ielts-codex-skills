## 流程图

### 单线型

Overall, it is a linear, man-made process in which **[输入物]** is turned into **[最终产物]**. The process begins with **[起始阶段]** and ends with **[最终阶段]**.



### 汇合型

Overall, this is a mainly linear, man-made process in which **[多种输入物]** are combined to produce **[混合物或中间产物]**. Once the materials have merged, the mixture passes through several stages before being turned into **[最终产物]**.

Overall, this is a mainly linear process with a separate branch producing **[副产物]** as a by-product. The main material passes through several stages before being turned into **[主要产物]**, while **[副产物]** is sent to **[用途或去向]**.

### 分支型 - 并列

Overall, this is a man-made process with two branches leading to different final products. It begins with **[输入物]** and then divides into two routes, producing **[产物 A]** and **[产物 B]** respectively.





### 循环型

Overall, this is a cycle consisting of **[阶段数量]** stages. It begins with **[起始阶段]** and ends with **[最终阶段]**, after which the cycle starts again.





### 并行型

Overall, this is a man-made process consisting of two parallel routes, one for **[对象 A]** and the other for **[对象 B]**. The two routes pass through separate stages before producing **[产物 A]** and **[产物 B]** respectively.
